Support Services

🕒 Alcohol Sales in Finland: You can buy alcoholic drinks (like beer, cider, and long drinks up to 5.5%) from grocery stores between 9 AM and 9 PM. For stronger alcohol, visit Alko, the state-owned liquor store. Bars and restaurants follow their own schedules.

Services by City

🦢 Helsinki

  1. 🚇 Transportation

    • Metro, tram, and bus: Monday–Sunday, 5:30 AM – 12:30 AM
    • Rautatientori Service Point: Monday–Friday, 9 AM – 5 PM

  2. 📶 Public Wi-Fi

    • Available 24/7 in city centers, libraries, and stations

  3. 🛒 Grocery Stores

    • K-Market, S-Market, Lidl: 7 AM – 11 PM (daily)

  4. 🍷 Alko (Alcohol Store)

    • City Center: Monday–Saturday, 9 AM – 8 PM (closed Sunday)

  5. 🔥 Saunas

    • Löyly: Daily, 10 AM – 10 PM
    • Allas Sea Pool: Daily, 7 AM – 9 PM
    • Kotiharjun Sauna: Tuesday–Sunday, 2 PM – 8 PM (closed Monday)

❄️ Oulu

  1. 🚇 Transportation

    • Bus and bike lanes: Monday–Sunday, 6 AM – 11 PM
    • City Center Transfer Point: Monday–Friday, 8 AM – 6 PM

  2. 📶 Public Wi-Fi

    • Available in parks, libraries, and shopping areas

  3. 🛒 Grocery Stores

    • Sale, Prisma, K-Citymarket: 8 AM – 10 PM

  4. 🍷 Alko (Alcohol Store)

    • Downtown Alko: Monday–Saturday, 9 AM – 8 PM (closed Sunday)

  5. 🔥 Saunas

    • Turkansaari Sauna: Weekends only, 4 PM – 9 PM
    • Merikoski Sauna: Daily, 2 PM – 8 PM

🏭 Tampere

  1. 🚇 Transportation

    • Tram and bus: Monday–Sunday, 5 AM – Midnight
    • City Info Center: Weekdays 9 AM – 6 PM

  2. 📶 Public Wi-Fi

    • Available in all city transport hubs and public libraries

  3. 🛒 Grocery Stores

    • S-Market, Lidl, Alepa: Open daily from 7 AM – 11 PM

  4. 🍷 Alko (Alcohol Store)

    • Koskikeskus Alko: Monday–Saturday, 9 AM – 8 PM

  5. 🔥 Saunas

    • Rauhaniemi Sauna: Open daily 2 PM – 9 PM
    • Rajaportti Sauna: Tuesday–Sunday, 3 PM – 9 PM

🌲 Espoo

  1. 🚇 Transportation

    • Metro and local buses: 6 AM – 11 PM daily
    • Espoo Transit Center: Monday–Friday, 8 AM – 6 PM

  2. 📶 Public Wi-Fi

    • Free city Wi-Fi available in shopping malls and libraries

  3. 🛒 Grocery Stores

    • K-Market, Sello Market: 7 AM – 11 PM

  4. 🍷 Alko (Alcohol Store)

    • Alko Iso Omena: Monday–Saturday, 9 AM – 8 PM

  5. 🔥 Saunas

    • Serena Sauna: Daily, 3 PM – 9 PM
    • Espoonlahti Sauna: Tuesday–Sunday, 4 PM – 8 PM
